<b>Definition:</b> A <b>CuratorialActivity</b> is activity a <b>Person</b> participate in as a custodian of a <b>CollectionObject</b>.<br/><b>Scope: </b>A <b>CuratorialActivity</b> may contain data about long term responsibility of a CollectionObject. It could also contain data about changes to the curation of an object.<br/><b>Context:</b> Multiple Persons can be associated with a single CuratorialActivity, but some activities can only have one related <b>Person</b>.<br/>This is the based on the <i>Activity </i>in the PROV 3-axiom schema. (with the <b>CollectionObject</b> being the <i>used Entity</i> and the <b>Person</b> the <i>wasAssociatedWith Agent</i>)<br/><b>Examples:</b> <br/><ul>
<li>Has/had (overall) responsibility for (Head Of Collection, Curator in Charge)</li><li>Labelled object.</li></ul>
**** Can change person to Agent and include Groups ****<br/>From group <b>Definition: </b>A <b>Group</b> is a named set of Persons and/or Groups that have collaborated in a joint association with another entity.<br/><b>Scope</b>: It may act as a <b>Party</b>, or just link people and other groups together.<br/><b>Context</b>: This could be an organisational unit or legally defined grouping<br/><b>Examples</b>: <br/><ul>
<li>Collaborators on a project</li><li>Team</li></ul>
