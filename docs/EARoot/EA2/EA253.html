<b>Definition:</b> This represents a physical instance in the collection of a single specific taxon. It may correspond to a single <b>CollectionObject</b>, be spread across multiple <b>CollectionObject</b>s, or represent part of a <b>CollectionObject</b>.<br/><b>Scope:</b> What that taxon is might be not fully known, or there may be different opinions from different people over time, so we allow for multiple <b>Determination</b>s with citations.<br/><b>Context: </b>Could be a representation of a physical instance. e.g. for a model<br/>
